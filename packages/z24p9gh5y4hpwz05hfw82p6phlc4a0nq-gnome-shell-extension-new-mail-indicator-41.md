# gnomeExtensions.new-mail-indicator

## üìù Description

New mail indicator.

 It checks the notifications in the message tray related to a new mail coming from your default email client, no extra configuration needed, very light extension. Click on the icon does toggle your email client window.

 *** Do not disable the notifications of your default email client!

 *** It is not a standalone mail checker, you need your email client to be running.
