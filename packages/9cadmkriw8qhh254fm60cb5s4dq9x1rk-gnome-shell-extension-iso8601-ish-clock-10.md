# gnomeExtensions.iso8601-ish-clock

## üìù Description

Overrides Gnome's clock format with ISO8601-inspired one.

Clock preferences in Date & Time section of Gnome Settings are respected, in a way.
"Date" and "Seconds" behave as usual.
"Week Day" inserts ISO Week and Weekday between the date and time. E.g. "2023-10-16 W42-1 19:45".
