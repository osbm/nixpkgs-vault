# gnomeExtensions.proton-vpn-button

Proton VPN button in top panel.

 Click on button to close/open Proton VPN app. Does not interfere in any way with connection itself: the extension just reads GNOME quick settings VPN button infos.

 Useful for those who don't want to use Ubuntu AppIndicators extension (Proton VPN in systray's legacy indicators).
